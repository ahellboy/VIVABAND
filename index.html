Welcome to VIVA's Rock World!

VIVA是一支高中生组成的摇滚乐队，成立于2023年，上海市宝山世外学校。我们用音乐点燃青春，传递热血与梦想！快来加入我们的音乐旅程吧！

VIVA is a rock band formed at BSWFL high school in 2023. We ignite youth with music and share passion and dreams! Join our musical journey!
